Our company history, and how we do things

A quick guide to our company history

I'm Elizabeth aka “Lizzy” Founder of Finance with Lizzy

If you are looking for the best finance tips, you have come to the right place.

I started out my professional career in London UK as a an Assistant Accountant for QBE Insurance (S&P 500 company), then moved to Lloyds Banking group in various Finance and Accounting manager roles. Since then, I have worked in the UK and also relocated overseas for other finance roles. Throughout the years I successfully completed all my Accountancy exams and become a Chartered Accountant.

I also have a 1st class honours BA degree in Accounting and Finance and an MSc in Accounting and Finance. After completing my professional accounting, I started to review my own personal finances and the amount of debt I had racked up.

What started as a personal interest in 2021 viewing and reducing my own debt, has turned into a business fuelled by my passion and love for finance and helping people with my lessons learned and tips with the beauty of all things finance.

The words "Finance with Lizzy” originates from a mix of my name and my love for finance. In the bible John the baptist's Mum was Elizabeth. Elizabeth simply means my God is abundance.

Finance with Lizzy is simply translated to Finance with Abundance.
